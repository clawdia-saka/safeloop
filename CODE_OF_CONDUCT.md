# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make SafeLoop a practical, respectful project for people building and
operating AI agent systems. Contributors and maintainers are expected to treat
one another with patience, clarity, and professional respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, documentation, and safety boundaries.
- Assume good intent while still taking security and safety concerns seriously.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, private run artifacts, or exploit details in
  public issues or pull requests.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations
may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
